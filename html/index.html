Volunteer

A one-of-a-kind opportunity to make an impact in the lives of those who need it most

As a team, we work to connect vulnerable individuals who are experiencing homelessness with valuable medical and mental health services. We help patients develop a medical care plan and navigate the healthcare system by assisting with appointment scheduling, providing transportation, accompanying patients to local medical services and advocating for patients during their appointments.

Medical Staff

Duties include, but are not limited to: Managing wound care and medical/diagnostic packs Recording inventory of supplies and dispensing medications upon clinician requests Checking the vitals signs of every patient who will be seen by a clinician and reporting them to the assigned CHW

Mental Health Clinician

Duties include, but are not limited to: Providing psychosocial assessment, including assessment for safety Offering therapeutic intervention to stabilize persons experiencing trauma or crisis Giving referrals to outpatient clinic or community resources

NOTE: Our patients and teams rely on consistent attendance. On average, any volunteer position requires about 4 hours of time each month depending on level of involvement. We ask that you consider this commitment fully before applying.

Office Support

Duties include, but are not limited to: Greeting patients Alleviating any patient concerns Assisting with hospitality items (water, socks, etc.). In the case of the client requiring medical attention, the office support staff gathers the chief complaint, assigns a CHW to the encounter and triages the patients based on medical priority.
